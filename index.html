Official Formula Reference
The 3 Formulas (verbatim from sss.gov.ph)
F1 = ₱300 + (20% × AMSC) + (2% × AMSC) × (CYS − 10) F2 = 40% × AMSC F3 = ₱1,200 if ≥10 CYS | ₱2,400 if ≥20 CYS Pension = MAX(F1, F2, F3) + ₱1,000 mandatory benefit
Source: sss.gov.ph/retirement-benefit (official SSS website, fetched June 2026). These are the exact formulas as published under RA 11199 Section 12.
AMSC — Average Monthly Salary Credit
AMSC = average of your 60 HIGHEST monthly MSCs in your entire contribution history Max AMSC for pension computation = ₱20,000 (MSC above ₱20K goes to MPF Pension Booster)
OFW minimum MSC is ₱8,000. You pay 15% yourself — no employer share. If your MSC history is mostly ₱10K–₱15K but you increase to ₱20K in the last 5 years (60 months), your AMSC rises significantly because SSS takes the 60 highest, not the last 60.
CYS — Credited Years of Service
CYS = Total posted monthly contributions ÷ 12 (rounded down to nearest whole year)
Every 12 months of posted contributions = 1 CYS. Gaps in contributions reduce CYS. The jump from 10 to 11 CYS activates the 2% bonus in Formula 1 — making years 11 onwards more valuable than years 1–10.
Add-ons (Guaranteed for All Pensioners)
+ ₱1,000/month mandatory benefit (RA 11199 §12-C) + 13th Month Pension every December + 10% per minor dependent child (max 5 children)
The ₱1,000 and 13th month are not optional — every qualifying pensioner receives them automatically regardless of formula result.
What Every OFW Needs to Know About SSS
⚡ 5 Critical Facts for OFWs in Singapore
⏱️
120 Months = Non-Negotiable MinimumYou need at least 120 posted monthly contributions to qualify for a lifetime monthly pension. Below that, SSS pays back your total contributions plus interest as a one-time lump sum only. 10 years is the floor — every gap hurts.
📈
Every Year After 10 Earns 2% MoreFormula 1 adds 2% of AMSC for every CYS beyond 10. A member at ₱20,000 AMSC earns ₱400/mo extra for each additional year past 10. Going from 10 to 30 years adds ₱8,000/mo to your pension — that is why consistency compounds.
⚠️
Pension Is Capped at ₱20,000 MSCPaying MSC above ₱20,000 does NOT increase your basic monthly pension. The excess goes into MPF (MySSS Pension Booster) — a separate savings fund paid out over a fixed term. Both are valuable but they are different pots of money.
🏝️
You Can Pay in Singapore via GCash or MayaOFWs can remit SSS contributions online through GCash, Maya, UnionBank, and other SSS-accredited channels. No need to go to the Philippines. You can also pay multiple months in advance to avoid gaps in your CYS record.
💸
SSS Is the Floor — Not the PlanAt max AMSC and 30 CYS, your pension is roughly ₱13,300/mo. For an OFW used to a Singapore salary, that will not sustain your lifestyle in retirement. Treat SSS as guaranteed base income, then build your investment portfolio on top of it.
